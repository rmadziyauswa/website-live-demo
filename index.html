Data Harmonization

When systems can't integrate,
the problem is classification.

Hypericum installs a semantic control plane across your enterprise data: governed domain intelligence that replaces fragmented taxonomies with versioned, auditable structure your systems can actually use.

The challenge

Most integration failures are not technical failures. They are classification failures that compound silently across every system and acquisition.

Post-acquisition system chaos
  • Acquired companies using incompatible classification systems
  • Product catalogs that cannot be reconciled
  • ERPs that will not communicate across entities
  • Duplicate records and conflicting master data

Integration delays costing millions. Synergies deferred or lost entirely.

Legacy integration debt
  • Past acquisitions never fully integrated at the data layer
  • Multiple divisions using different supplier taxonomies
  • Siloed analytics that cannot produce a consolidated view
  • AI deployments degrading silently on inconsistent inputs

Reporting overhead, analytical blind spots, and AI that underperforms without explanation.
